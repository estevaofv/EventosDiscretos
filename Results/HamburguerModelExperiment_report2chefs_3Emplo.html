HamburguerModelExperiment Report

Model HamburguerModel

Description
This is the Vancarrier_1st_P_Model description,which means it is the first VanCarrier model in a process oriented version. This model is a service station model located at a container harbour. Conatinertrucks will arrive and require the loading of a container. A Vancarrier (VC) is on duty and will head off to find the required container in the storage. He will then deliver the container to the truck. The truck then leaves the area.In case the VC is busy, the truck waits for his turn on the parking-lot.If the VC is idle, it waits on his own parking spot for the truck to come.
